Experience

The following includes the biggest highlights during university as well as my work experience!

Veritas

As part of my software engineering course at DCU, I worked at Veritas for six months with a great team of Agile software developers. My work involved five different stories where I added functionality to a few different products. I took part in daily standups, bimonthly retrospectives, meetings, presentations and two deployments with teams in India and the USA.

4th Year project - Agent Based Model

My 4th year project (with another student) was an agent based model (ABM) that simulated the relationship between crime and inequality. We made use of MASON which was an ABM framework to help us with the simulation. We implemented several algorithms to calculate standard deviation, gini coeficient, income, expenditure, hunger, stress, etc. There was also a lot of research done for the topic as it was a simulation.

Shadowing a network engineer

During my 1st/2nd year summer holidays, I shadowed a network engineer and helped him with various tasks. We set up a LAN for two apartment blocks and I separately did trouble shooting for students when they had problems getting an internet signal or were having network issues.

Agile Academic Publication

The biggest highlight of my 4th year was achieving a publication at a conference in Dusseldorf as well as the best research paper of the year - "Agile Software Development – Do We Really Calculate the Costs? A Multivocal Literature Review". I achieved this publication with a team of three other students. The work involved reading, analysing, summarising and extracting information from about 150 research papers, and formulating this into the publication.

3rd Year Project - Java App

For my 3rd year project (with another student) we developed a Java app that enabled the digitisation and encryption of ID's. It revolved around the idea of sending data from one phone to another through the use of NFC. I used Firebase to store client data and sent that data to another phone for verification.

BRS Golf tester

For my school transition year, I worked as a website tester for BRS golf.
