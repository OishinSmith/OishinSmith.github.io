Experience

The following includes the biggest highlights during University as well as my work experience!

Veritas

As part of my software engineering course at DCU, I worked at Veritas for six months as part of a great team of agile software developers. My work involed five different stories where I added functionality to a few different products. I took part in daily standups, bimonthly retrospectives, meetings, presentations and two deployments with teams in India and the USA.

4th year project

My fourth year project was an Agent based model (ABM) that simulated the relationship between crime and inequality. we made use of MASON, which was an ABM framework to help us out with the simulation. We implemented several algorithms to calculate standard deviation, gini coeficient, income, expenditure, hunger, stress, etc. There was also alot of research done for the topic as it was a simulation.

Shadowing a network engineer

During my summer holidays, I shadowed a network engineer and helped him out with various tasks. I set up a LAN for two apartment blocks and separetely did some trouble shooting for students when they had problems getting an internet signal or were having network issues.

Academic Publication

During one of my 4th year modules a team of 4 students which included me, had to write an academic paper on a particular topic. It took us 3-4 months to write the paper as it included careful planning, research and editing. After we had finished our exams, our lecturer congratulated us as the paper had been of such a good quality that it was going to be published with the help of the lecturer and other academic publishers.

3rd year project

For my third year project I made an app, written in java, that revolved around the idea of sending data from one phone to another throught the use of NFC. I used firebase to store client data and sent that data to another phone for verification through NFC.

BRS Golf Web Developer

I worked as a website developer for BRS golf during my transition year (4th year secondary school). I was mainly there to test websites and find bugs with the system.
